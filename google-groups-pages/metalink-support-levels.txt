<h1>metalink-support-levels</h1>
<p>Because of Metalink's <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Metalink#Metalink_client_feature_comparison">wide variety of features</a>, it might be useful to name certain sets of features.<br> </p><p><br> </p><p><font size="4">Level 1 - Minimal Metalink Implementation</font><br></p><p><br>Possible Names - Metalink Lite, Metalink Minimal, Metalink Simple</p><p>Current clients at this level: TheWorld Browser, SmartFTP, Metadl, DLApplet, Free Download Manager</p><p><br></p><p>Client Requirements:<br></p><ul><li>multi-file downloading<br></li><li>HTTP or FTP protocol&nbsp;</li><li>one source or more (segmented multi-source downloading not required)</li><li>subdirectory creation</li></ul><p><font size="4">Level 2 - Typical Metalink Implementation</font></p><p>&nbsp;</p><p>Possible Names -&nbsp; Metalink Basic, Metalink Typical, just plain "Metalink"<br></p><p>Current clients at this level: DownThemAll, GetRight, KGet, Orbit Downloader, Net Transport, Phex, Speed Download, wxDownload Fast </p><p>&nbsp;</p><p>Client Requirements:</p><ul><li>Meet all Level 1 requirements&nbsp;</li><li>resume downloads from where they left off between sessions</li><li>at least MD5/SHA-1 whole file checksumming</li><li>download a single file from multiple sources simultaneously (segmented multi-source downloading)</li><li>HTTP and FTP support
</li></ul><p><br></p><p><font size="4">Level 3 - Complete Metalink Implementation</font><br></p><p>&nbsp;</p><p>Possible Names - Metalink Full, Metalink Complete<br></p><p>Current clients at this level: Metalink Checker, GGet </p><p>&nbsp;</p><p>Client Requirements:</p><ul><li>Meet all Level 2 requirements<br></li><li>Checks chunk/piece checksums (MD5 or SHA1)<br></li><li>Awareness of all of the following tags: location, OS, language<br></li><li>Ability to check at least one advanced checksum, SHA-256, PGP, etc.</li><li>HTTP with Transparent Negotiation</li></ul><p><br></p><p><font size="4">Level 4 - Peer to Peer Support</font><br>&nbsp;</p><p>Possible Names - Metalink P2P, Metalink Advanced<br></p><p>Current clients at this level: aria2, Retriever<br></p><p><br></p><p></p><p>Client Requirements:</p>
<ul><li>Meet all Level 3 requirements</li><li>Implement at least one P2P protocol (Bittorrent, Magnet)<br>
  </li></ul><br><br><p>Many clients may never support P2P or digital signatures. Where do those fit in? <br></p><wbr>
